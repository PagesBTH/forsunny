p5.js Code for my House

Reflection


I knew about p5.js from my Pattern Designs class. However I never really dealt with p5 in the context of drawing, so I wanted to do something simple like a house. I got the inspiration from Millie's Math House, so I just wanted to draw some simple shapes like circles, squares, rectangles and triangles. Colours were also simple to implement, so I decided to colour the shapes as well. I really liked this lab, and I would do it again for fun!

I wanted to draw my house pretty simple. I created the necessary shapes, (triangle for the house, rectangles for the body, doors, and light fixture, circles for the moon and light bulb). When I drew the shapes, I had a bit of trouble aligning the shapes perfectly onto the page, so I had to pick a random number, and then figure out how many pixels I need to shift the shape itself. Colouring was really easy thankfully due to the fill() function. Once I drew the shapes and coloured them I tested it on my local machine and it works!
