Description


This was a video that I had a lot of fun making. I know the assignment mentioned to include my voice, but I wanted to take it a step further. I wanted to learn a lot about Adobe Premiere editing, so I took some clips that I had from recording Lab 4, and went to town on it. I included text to show my thoughts when I was playing, along with text for my opponent. I wanted to show you my thought process when I play fighting games. I didn't just want to include text alone, so I made a bunch of memes that I found really funny and included them in here. Most of them are sorta relevant to how I feel when I play. Audio was kinda easy to do. Premiere includes an audio section where you can change, modify and cut audio right in the software, instead of going into Audacity. Most of it though was unedited, other than some minor pitch changes and audio level differences. I found this assignment really fun, and it helped me learn a new skill : video editing.
