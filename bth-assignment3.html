Assignment 3

Golf Ball
























Bouncing Ball

Reflection


Working on assignment 3 was quite interesting. I wanted to make a project that would interest me a lot, and working with margins was the idea. I wanted to make a simple golf ball that would bounce around the canvas. I used some simple math to calculate where the borders are for a canvas, and then made two simple if statements (one for the x-axis, and y-axis). In terms of interactivity, I wanted to keep it simple, and display where our mouse is located on the canvas. This assignment was kinda difficult, mainly cause I haven't really written p5 code that animates. Getting the golf ball to properly bounce wasn't really the hard part. The hard part was getting the ball to move in the first place! I'd rate this a 6/10.
